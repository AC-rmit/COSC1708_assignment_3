Scope and Limits

At the beginning of this assignment, we had no illusions about completing this app in the time we had left in the semester. The short answer is no, we couldn't, not within the current timeframe. To complete this app, we realised that going through all the requisite skills learning and work to make a tangible prototype would take far too long, considering what other tasks we had with our other courses and outside university life. In the deliverables we mentioned our aims and how we would achieve them. Our final vision saw stand-alone navigation software, capable of downloading datasets, identifying and pulling the required data, integrating the data with Google Maps API, liaising with government software to initiate parking space bookings and seamlessly presenting it in a visually appealing format.
Realistically, in the 6 weeks we had to develop has much as we could, we decided that three IT artefacts could be developed in this time period, with integrating all of them to be a remote possibility at the end.
